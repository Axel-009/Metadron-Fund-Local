## LAYER 2: SIGNAL PROCESSING вҖ” MacroEngine

### MacroEngine (`engine/signals/macro_engine.py`)
**Purpose**: Regime classification + GMTF money velocity analysis

#### Regime Classification
```
MarketRegime: BULL | BEAR | TRANSITION | STRESS | CRASH
CubeRegime:   TRENDING | RANGE | STRESS | CRASH
```

**Classification logic**:
- VIX > 35 вҶ’ CRASH
- VIX > 28 AND SPY_3M < -10% вҶ’ STRESS
- SPY_1M > 3% AND SPY_3M > 5% вҶ’ BULL
- SPY_1M < -3% AND SPY_3M < -5% вҶ’ BEAR
- Else вҶ’ TRANSITION

#### GMTF (Global Monetary Tension Framework)
**SDR basket weights** (IMF 2022): USD=0.4338, EUR=0.2931, CNY=0.1228, JPY=0.0759, GBP=0.0744

**Base tension**: `Оё = (M2/GDP) Г— (1+unemployment) Г— GDP_growth`

**4 Non-linear gamma multipliers**:
| Gamma | Trigger | Multiplier |
|-------|---------|------------|
| О“_Liquidity | M2 growth > 2Г— GDP growth | 1 + sigmoid Г— 1.5 |
| О“_FX | USD yield spike > 25bps | 1 - sigmoid Г— 0.5 |
| О“_Wage | Unemployment < 4.2% | 1 + sigmoid Г— 2.0 |
| О“_Reserve | USD share < 57% | 1 + sigmoid Г— 1.5 |

**Sigmoid**: `Пғ(x) = 1 / (1 + exp(-15 Г— (x - threshold)))`

**Aggregation**: `GMTF = ОЈ(SDR_weight Г— Оё Г— О“_L Г— О“_W Г— О“_FX Г— О“_S)`, smoothed with 5-day rolling mean

#### Sub-Modules (7 total)

1. **MoneyVelocityModule** вҖ” Fisher V=GDP/M2, credit impulse, TED spread, SOFR tracking
   - Liquidity Score 0-100: velocity(25%) + credit_impulse(20%) + TED(20%) + VIX(20%) + yield_curve(15%)

2. **SectorRanker** вҖ” Macro-adjusted momentum ranking
   - Composite = `Sharpe Г— regime_multiplier Г— (1 + relative_strength Г— 0.5)`
   - Blended momentum: 50% 3M + 30% 1M + 20% 6M
   - Regime multipliers: BULL cyclicals Г—1.25, STRESS defensives Г—1.4
   - Factor rotation: MOMENTUM | VALUE | QUALITY | DEFENSIVE | NEUTRAL

3. **CarryToVolatility** вҖ” G10 FX carry-to-vol signals
   - `CtV = carry_spread / realised_vol(FX)`
   - Gate: CtV > 0.5 AND no stop-loss active
   - Stop-loss: vol spike > 2Пғ in 1 day
   - SDR-weighted aggregate CtV

4. **RegimeTransitionDetector** вҖ” Markov transition with hysteresis (3 days)
   - Base transition probabilities (5Г—5 matrix)
   - Confidence = 60% frequency + 40% base probability

5. **YieldCurveAnalyzer** вҖ” 2s10s, 3m10y, term premium, real rate
   - Curve shapes: STEEP | FLAT | INVERTED | BEAR_FLAT | BULL_STEEP
   - Recession probability via NY Fed probit: `P = ОҰ(-0.5333 - 0.6330 Г— spread_3m10y)`

6. **CreditPulseMonitor** вҖ” HY-IG spread, z-score, credit impulse
   - Thresholds: NORMAL(<3.0) | ELEVATED(3.0-4.5) | STRESS(4.5-6.0) | CRISIS(>8.0)

7. **MacroFeatureBuilder** вҖ” 50+ features for ML
   - VIX(5) + Equity(8) + Yield(8) + Credit(5) + Commodity(6) + Momentum(8) + Volatility(5) + Regime(7+)
