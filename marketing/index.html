About

Engineered where complexity meets conviction.

Academic research has long documented that market inefficiencies are not random. They are driven by behavioural persistence, informational asymmetry, and institutional constraints that create predictable patterns of mispricing. Most investment firms acknowledge this. Very few have built the infrastructure to act on it systematically. Metadron Capital was founded on a single premise: that these inefficiencies can be identified, classified, and harvested through superior architecture.


Our approach replaces discretionary judgement with a rigorous, multi-layered validation process. Every position is the output of quantitative models that interrogate macro conditions, factor exposures, regime state, and portfolio-level risk before capital moves. The result is a portfolio constructed by evidence, not opinion.

Vision

A quantitative framework for capital.

We believe the next generation of outperformance will come from platforms that learn and adapt in real time. Markets evolve. Regimes shift. Factor premia rotate. A static allocation framework cannot capture the dynamics of a living system. Metadron's vision is a continuously improving investment architecture that becomes more precise with every market cycle it observes.

01
Conviction Over Consensus
Capital is allocated only when multiple independent signals converge. No single indicator, however strong, is sufficient to generate a position. This convergence requirement dramatically reduces false positives and concentrates risk in the highest probability outcomes.
02
Structural Edge Extraction
Every trade originates from a documented market inefficiency. The system classifies and scores opportunities across mean-reversion, event-driven, credit dislocation, and contagion dynamics. When conditions shift, capital reallocates before the thesis breaks.
03
Five Sleeves, Zero Ego
Portfolio construction follows a regime-aware tactical allocation model. Sleeve weightings shift dynamically based on state variables including momentum, volatility, credit spreads, and liquidity conditions. The allocation is objective, measurable, and fully attributable.

Strategy

Multi-asset, cross-market, relentless.

Our investment process is designed to extract returns from structural market phenomena that most participants either misunderstand or lack the infrastructure to capture. Each strategy sleeve targets a distinct source of return, ensuring genuine diversification at the portfolio level rather than correlated bets labelled differently.

01
Liquidity Plumbing
We decompose central bank balance sheets, dealer reserve flows, Treasury General Account movements, and collateral velocity to map where liquidity is accumulating before it surfaces in price. Positioning ahead of these flows has been a consistent source of early-cycle alpha.
02
Carry vs. Volatility
Carry strategies are normalised against realised and implied volatility to isolate genuine risk premium from disguised duration exposure. This distinction, overlooked by most systematic strategies, has materially improved the portfolio's risk-adjusted return profile.
03
Event-Driven & Dislocations
Merger arbitrage, distressed credit, spinoffs, activist situations, and contingent value rights are modelled across twelve event categories. Our quantitative approach identifies the dislocation window and sizes positions to capture the information asymmetry at its widest point.
04
Regime Intelligence
A proprietary regime classifier distinguishes between trending, ranging, stress, and crash environments with sub-daily resolution. A contagion graph maps systemic linkages so that when correlations spike and traditional diversification fails, our allocation has already rotated to a defensive posture.
05
Execution & Kill Switch
Position sizing follows a Kelly-optimised framework bounded by portfolio-level drawdown gates and a strict beta corridor. An automated circuit breaker activates during periods of systemic stress, reducing gross exposure before losses compound. Capital preservation in tail events is not a feature. It is a design principle.

The Intelligence Layer

Where quantitative intelligence meets cross-asset discipline.

Faster Than the Narrative
Our models process thousands of securities across the 11 GICS sectors in real time. By the time a narrative forms around a market move, the Cube has already identified the signal, validated it against regime conditions, and repositioned accordingly. Speed without accuracy is reckless. We have both.
Adaptive, Not Static
The platform continuously retrains its signal weightings as market conditions evolve. What worked in a low-volatility carry regime will not work in a stress-driven deleveraging cycle. Our architecture recognises these transitions and adapts allocation weightings accordingly, keeping the portfolio aligned with where the opportunity set actually is.
Every Dollar Has a Reason
Every position in the portfolio carries full attribution from signal origination through to execution. Investors can trace each trade to a specific market condition, a specific model output, and a specific risk budget. This level of transparency is rare in quantitative strategies and central to our investor reporting.
The Map Rewrites Itself
Alpha decays. Every successful strategy attracts capital and compresses its own returns over time. The Cube is designed to detect this decay and continuously scan for the next generation of mispricings before they become crowded. This self-renewing quality is what separates a platform from a strategy.

Structural inefficiencies are persistent, repeatable, and engineerable but only for platforms with the architecture to observe the full surface. The next decade of outperformance will belong to those who built the infrastructure to capture what others cannot see.

The Story

From trading desks to engineered alpha.

The Foundation
The desk.
The early career was spent on institutional desks at Dodge & Cox and Bank of America Merrill Lynch, managing capital across rates, credit, and multi-asset mandates. The lesson that emerged was not about markets. It was about the people trading them. Discretionary bias, narrative addiction, and herd behaviour produce inefficiencies that repeat with remarkable regularity.
The Insight
Signal, not story.
The conviction crystallised after years of watching talented traders lose money to their own psychology. Markets do not reward the smartest narrative. They reward disciplined, systematic extraction of mispricing. The question became: what if we could build an architecture that removes the human weaknesses while preserving the human insights?
The Build
Engineering the Cube.
That question became the Metadron Cube. Built from scratch over two years of development and backtesting, the platform integrates regime-aware tactical allocation with automated capital preservation. Every design decision was informed by real experience of what breaks when markets turn.
The Mission
The mission.
Metadron Capital exists to answer a question that the traditional asset management industry has avoided: can a small, focused team with superior technology consistently outperform larger institutions burdened by bureaucracy, career risk, and legacy infrastructure? Our track record is the beginning of that answer.
